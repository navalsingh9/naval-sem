"Does Work-Life Balance directly reduce employee attrition, or does its effect run entirely through Job Satisfaction and Manager Trust?"

"Does Perceived Usefulness directly drive MOOC completion, or does the effect collapse under Social Belonging and Self-Efficacy — and which construct is the real dropout lever?"

Expected path coefficients

Pathβt-statp-valueSignificant?
Perceived Ease of Use → Perceived Usefulness0.447.1<0.001✓ Yes
